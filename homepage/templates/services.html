What We Take

SWMS makes recycling easy and accessible across Central Massachusetts. As a mobile redemption service, we pick up eligible containers directly from your home, institution, or business. Our mission is to reduce waste, promote recycling, and help residents reclaim deposits from redeemable containers.

We accept the following types of containers for recycling:

  • Bottles
  • Aluminium Cans
  • Tin Cans
  • Steel Cans
  • Malt Beverages Bottles

Aluminum Cans

In Massachusetts, aluminum cans, such as those for soda and beer, are eligible for redemption and should be marked with "MA 5¢" or a similar deposit indication.

Glass Bottles

In Massachusetts, certain glass bottles, such as those used for beer, malt beverages, and sparkling water qualify for redemption if marked with "MA 5¢". Wine and liquor bottles are generally not eligible for redemption under Massachusetts law, except in certain local programs.

Check the Label on the Container

Look for the following markings to confirm the container is eligible for redemption in Massachusetts:

  • "MA 5¢" (Massachusetts Redemption)

These markings indicate that the container qualifies for deposit refunds in participating states.

FAQs

What does the "MA 5¢" label mean?

This label indicates that the container is eligible for redemption in Massachusetts. Consumers pay a deposit of 5 cents when they purchase beverages in marked containers. This deposit can be refunded when the container is returned to a participating redemption center.

How can I get my 5¢ deposit back?

You can get your refund by returning eligible containers to a redemption center or through SWMS's convenient pickup service in Central Massachusetts. Schedule a pickup with SWMS, and we'll collect your recyclables right from your doorstep. We process payments promptly after collection, making it easy for you to get your deposit back without leaving home.
