FAQs

What does the "MA 5¢" label mean?

This label indicates that the container is eligible for redemption in Massachusetts. Consumers pay a deposit of 5 cents when they purchase beverages in marked containers. This deposit can be refunded when the container is returned to a participating redemption center.

How can I get my 5¢ deposit back?

You can get your refund by returning eligible containers to a redemption center or through 's convenient pickup service in Central Massachusetts. Schedule a pickup with , and we'll collect your recyclables right from your doorstep. We process payments promptly after collection, making it easy for you to get your deposit back without leaving home.
