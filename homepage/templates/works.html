How It Works

Residents of Central Massachusetts can now recycle with ease using the SWMS mobile redemption service. No more hassle of transporting bags of cans and bottles to distant redemption centers. Our service brings recycling right to your doorstep!

We provide collection bins for your convenience. With these, you can sort your redeemable containers, including aluminum cans and glass bottles. Just schedule a pickup through our app, place your items out for collection, and you're all set. We'll handle the collection and processing, making recycling effortless for you.

Our processing center schedules drivers to collect at your chosen time. Your items are then recycled within 24 hours of pickup. We sort your items, calculate your redemption based on Massachusetts rates, and process your payment. You can choose to receive your redemption funds electronically or as a mailed check, with a small service fee to support our operations.
