Who Are We ?

Smart Waste Management System is a full-service, community-based Redemption Center (RC), offering an innovative approach to redemption.

As a redemption center company, SWMS provides a convenient, accessible, and streamlined option for returning deposit containers, such as redeemable cans and bottles. We serve residents, businesses, institutions, and municipalities, making the process effortless and entirely free of charge.

Our goal is to make recycling easier and more efficient, contributing to a cleaner environment and supporting local communities.
